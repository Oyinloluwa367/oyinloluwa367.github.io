Who she is

I'm Oyinloluwa — a business analyst, startup enthusiast, creative thinker, occasional overthinker, and someone deeply fascinated by people, ideas, ambition, and expression.

Instead of separating the professional from the personal, the analytical from the artistic, I decided to build a space where all of it can coexist. This is that space.

You'll find thoughts on startups and venture capital alongside paintings, sketches, personal essays, and spontaneous ideas. Small pieces of life as they evolve in real time.
